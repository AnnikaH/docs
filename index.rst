IncludeOS
=========

**IncludeOS** is an includable, minimal library operating system for C++ services running in the cloud or on devices. Starting a program with ``#include <os>``, will literally include a whole operating system into your service during link-time.

For more information: Visit our `website <https://www.includeos.org>`__, check out the project on `GitHub <https://github.com/includeos/IncludeOS>`__ or come chat with us on `Slack <https://goo.gl/NXBVsc>`__!
